'Interactive Story Part 1'

Description

For my interactive story assignment, I called it “Run to Live!” The story starts with two characters called Sunshine, a light blue cat with a sunshine birthmark on her face. And Pencoon, a raccoon + penguin creature with a top hat. And they are being chased by a monster who I dubbed Sprinkles, a dough-like creature with sprinkles. The reason he is chasing them is because they stole his sweets while he was sleeping. The choices I would give to the player are standard; you must choose whether you want them to continue or to stay put and get eaten which is an automatic (You Dead!)

But if you did press to continue, Sunshine and Pencoon, they are faced with a haunted house, and it is up to you whether you want to into the haunted house of go around. If you choose to go inside, they will be faced with a different monster depending on which room you choose. And it is up to you whether they face the monster, try to escape, or let them eat you, whilst also being mindful of the first monster that you were run from.
