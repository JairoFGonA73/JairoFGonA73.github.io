'Alter Photograph'

The image I chose is a blank family frame with the background of a blossom tree. The reason I chose this was that I needed a family frame. I could not find the exact image I had in mind, so I chose this one because the background was a nice touch. As for the image, I did not alter anything other than what was inside the frame. For a bit of background information, the characters you see are some of my OC’s. This is actually the first time I colored them, so I had a bit of fun with the colors. Like in the middle, we have Ami, a luna cat, and on her left is Mr.Puz, an egg that can hatch into anything, though only four creatures at a time. Then we have Crazy Stache on her left, and Haco, a living fire. On the top left is Tooth, a sorta of aquatic creature/plant, top middle is C.G or Chicken Ghost, and lastly we have Udia, a literal Sun.
